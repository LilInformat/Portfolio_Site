    Summary

    What inspires me to be an engineer are the stories of my deceased uncle who lived in poverty. He loved to learn and would starve himself to save money to buy himself books. Better yet, he would salvage whatever he can find in junkyards to build his own electronics which he couldn't afford. As a result, I spent a large part of my childhood breaking into electronics just to see the inside of it and then reassembling it. In 2016, I received my Electrical Engineering degree at the University of Toronto. During my studies, I learned to code in C/C++ as well as C# and WPF in group projects. My fourth year design project is a C# WPF application that determines a person's Big Five personality traits by recording how the person looks on the screen when shown a series of pictures. This application was trained on previous experimental results on student volunteers from campus. I am most experienced in programming with C/C++ having had a internship at AMD where I took part in designing, implementing and testing a feature for AMD crimson. The purpose of this feature was to improve customer experience when playing their favorite games. Additionally, I am familiar with programming in Python as well as developing with HTML, CSS and using Bootstrap. Currently, I am looking for an opportunity to further develop my skills as a programmer. I am interested in companies that operate in all industries and my main concern is the work environment. I'm looking for a place where there are many opportunities for learning, creativity and mentorship.
